About

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    From a simple hobby in the garage emerged a burgeoning demand for custom woodworking items. Initially crafted as gifts, word-of-mouth referrals propelled the hobby into a full-fledged business.

  • March 2011

    A Business is Born

    The inception of this business began as a part-time pursuit, born out of a passion for woodworking and a desire to indulge in something enjoyable.

  • December 2015

    Transition to Full Service

    As demand soared, it became evident that transitioning this endeavor into my full-time occupation was imperative to meet the growing needs. To ensure seamless operation and accommodate the influx of requests, I made the decision to expand the business and assemble a dedicated team of craftsmen.

  • July 2020

    Phase Two Expansion

    With the invaluable support of our expanded team and your unwavering encouragement, we're poised to take the next step in our journey. We're excited to announce plans for further expansion of our workshop to meet the ever-increasing demand for our craftsmanship. Your support has been instrumental in our success, and we extend our heartfelt gratitude as we embark on this exciting new chapter. Thank you for being a part of our story.
